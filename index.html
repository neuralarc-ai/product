02The Gap 002 / 012
What the market still hasn't solved

Enterprises are marrying one model
and paying for the wedding every day.

The current AI stack forces single-vendor exclusivity, fragments context across every prompt, ignores team structure, refuses to integrate with legacy systems, and leaves no audit trail. Five gaps. Every one of them ours to close.

i.
Single-LLM lock-in
Enterprise exclusivity deals with OpenAI, Anthropic, or Microsoft force every task, trivial or strategic, through one heavyweight model. Token costs balloon. Dependency deepens. Switching becomes painful.
ii.
Context is never persistent
Your business context resets at every prompt, every chat, every tool. Teams re-explain who they are, what they do, and what they've decided, thousands of times a week.
iii.
No team-grade collaboration
No major LLM gives a team a coherent shared knowledge base with role-based access. Mid-market and enterprise both suffer. Without shared context, AI output quality collapses.
iv.
Integration is a closed garden
Custom integration with legacy ERPs, CRMs, internal databases, and industry-specific tools is shallow or non-existent. AI lives in a sandbox; the business lives outside it.
v.
Zero governance, zero audit, growing shadow AI
Current platforms cannot tell you which prompt referenced which document, which employee did what, or whether sensitive data left the building. Meanwhile, employees use unsanctioned AI on personal accounts. A compliance time bomb already ticking in every enterprise.
